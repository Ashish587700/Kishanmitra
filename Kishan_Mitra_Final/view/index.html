About Kishan Mitra

Kishan Mitra is a revolutionary platform that connects farmers directly with buyers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices for agricultural produce.

Our mission is to empower farmers with technology, market access, and fair trade practices. We help farmers showcase their produce, find the right buyers, and negotiate better prices.

For buyers, we provide access to fresh, quality produce directly from farms, ensuring transparency, traceability, and fair pricing.
